About Us

Discover Nile Park Lodge

Nestled on the banks of the magnificent Nile River in Jinja, Nile Park Lodge offers a unique blend of comfort, elegance, and natural beauty. Our lodge is designed to give guests an unforgettable experience with stunning views of the Nile and lush, green surroundings.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat, an adventure-filled getaway, or a venue for your special events, Nile Park Lodge provides the perfect setting with our modern facilities and warm, personalized service.

Source of the Nile

Camping & Short Stays
